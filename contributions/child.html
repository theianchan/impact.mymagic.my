Entrepreneurs Dialogue

As every good entrepreneur knows, before you can start working on the solution, you need to define the problems.

In June, we organised a number of get-togethers for local entrepreneurs to discuss the strengths and weeknesses of the local ecosystem, the challenges they were facing personally, and the kind of help they were looking for.
The result was a series of engaging, insightful discussions that united the community in facing common issues, and set the tone for MaGIC’s programs in year one.

MaGIC’s coworking space has hosted over 120 events

One regular event is called Grill or Chill.

A session dedicated to our in-house startup community, Grill or Chill is an opportunity to learn about pitching and business from the brave startups who work at MaGIC's coworking space - who will present their business model and get grilled.

Fun fact? Grill or Chill was formerly called Grill AND Chill, and was almost universally mistaken for a barbeque session by first-time attendees.
