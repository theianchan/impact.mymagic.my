MSA Launch & MaGIC Academy

Knowledge is the foundation of success.

Following the General Assembly model, we’re building an international, scalable, and diverse education platform to give Malaysians the skills needed to build successful, world-class startups.

We kicked of MaGIC Academy with the MSA Launch event, a five-day journey that combined keynote speeches, workshops, investor speed dating sessions, and the largest startup career fair ever held in Malaysia.

Since then, the academy has only continued to grow.
