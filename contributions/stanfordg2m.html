Stanford Go2Market

Ever had a business idea you wanted to bring to market but didn't know how?

We invited 5 Stanford faculty members to Cyberjaya to help promising would-be entrepreneurs develop their business ideas over a 5-day series of classes. The students were high-potential individuals ready to commercialise their intellectual property, ideas, and research.

The classes covered design thinking, business models and strategy, customer value propositions, pricing strategy, financial reporting, and pitching.

Why Stanford Go2Market?

Stanford Go2Market is 3rd of three programs as part of MaGIC’s 3-year partnership with Stanford University’s Graduate School of Business
