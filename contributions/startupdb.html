Startup DB

A thriving startup ecosystem deserves to be properly catalogued.

This is important for investors and the public to track the number of high growth startups in a country, records of funding raised, exit history, and their progress along their journey. We built the definitive database of the Malaysian startup ecosystem, allowing anyone to get information about the companies, people, products, news & events that matter.

Towards this goal, MaGIC formed a partnership with CrunchBase, the world’s largest startup database

This partnership will give users of StartupDB access to CrunchBase’s rich data, as well as expose local startups to the world stage via CrunchBase’s platform.

The Startup DB will make it easier for MaGIC and other agencies to form a baseline for and measure the growth of startups, as well as track the overall expansion of the startup ecosystem.
