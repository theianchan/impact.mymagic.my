SEHATI

Empowering local communities

It is a time of galactic unrest. Social and environmental problems grow unchecked, and the nation needs its social entrepreneurs to take on the challenge, but their numbers are few.

A small band of entrepreneurs have set out to train promising individuals in the ways of social entrepreneurship, and awaken in them skills of business model development and social awareness...

"Baru saya nampak kepentingan social entrepreneurship ni dan banyak bidang yg boleh diceburi. Memulakan SE comes from the heart with the intention to make someone's life better. If you can't help 1,000 people, try change one person's life." Hassanah Hassan, SEHATI participant

The MaGIC team rounded up 20 experienced social entrepreneurs and 223 brave volunteers

And hit the road to bring a series of two-day workshops to communities all over the country. The social entrepreneurs shared stories of their struggles and successes with participants, who learned the basics of starting a social enterprise along the way.

The effort ended up reaching some of our largest crowds ever

After two months, the social entrepreneurs had spread the word to 3,215 people, journeyed through 6 states, secured 15 state and national partnerships...

And put on a showcase at SEHATI KL

Following the program, 30 SEHATI participants emerged with ideas to start their own social enterprises

For many, it was a life changing experience
