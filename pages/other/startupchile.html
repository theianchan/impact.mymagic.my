Signed an MoU with Startup Chile

The Startup Chile initiative is known internationally as a runaway success for its role in building a thriving ecosystem for tech startups in the heart of Chile.

We visited our friends in Chile to understand the impact their programs have had on the Chilean and Latin American economies, and we will be adapting the Startup Chile Accelerator model for the ASEAN region. The MaGIC Accelerator Program will see 50 startups from the ASEAN region coming to MaGIC in the second half of 2015 to develop and scale their businesses in Kuala Lumpur.
