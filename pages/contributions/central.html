MaGIC Central

If you’re starting a startup in Malaysia, where do you go?

MaGIC Central aims to provide a rich ‘one-stop shop’ database of resources from 76 government agencies and over 180 private organizations for startups at any stage, as well as facilitate virtual and physical interaction between entrepreneurs and agencies.

Visitors can find information on

Funding or grant agencies

Coworking spaces

Accelerators, incubators

Development assistance

and all sorts of public and private sector programs for students, startups, and aspiring founders.



Users can book online or in-person office hours with MaGIC staff and agencies who can direct them to the right resources
