MaGIC Bumipreneur

Past initiatives to grow the startup ecosystem focused largely on urban centers.

This overlooked a large pool of potential entrepreneurial talent in suburban and rural areas. MaGIC Bumipreneur is one of many initiatives to increase bumiputera participation by encouraging the growth of more role models, outreach programs and capacity building.

Watch the MaGIC Bumipreneur launch

1 MoU signed with Teraju, to conduct a technology adoption workshop for participants of the Superb challenge

1 MoU signed with INSKEN, to provide bootcamp content for early-stage entrepreneurs in the pre-revenue or post-funding or stage

MaGIC is also collaborating with

Dewan Perniagaan Melayu Malaysia

The State Agencies of Terengganu, Sabah, Sarawak

The New Entrepreneurs Foundation

And Generasi Muda Berjaya

To conduct social media marketing and branding workshops for bumiputera participants, open new coworking spaces, and bring participants into MaGIC’s upcoming accelerator program
